Glute Stretches
Seated Figure-Four Stretch: Sit on the floor with both legs extended. Bend one knee and place the ankle over the opposite thigh. Gently press the bent knee downward to deepen the stretch in the glute of the crossed leg.
----
Standing Glute Stretch: Stand tall and cross one ankle over the opposite knee. Slowly bend the standing leg and hinge forward at the hips, keeping your back straight to stretch the glute of the crossed leg.
----
Pigeon Pose: From a plank position, bring one knee forward and place it behind your wrist while extending the opposite leg straight back. Lower your hips toward the ground to stretch the glute of the bent leg.
----
Cross-Body Glute Stretch: Stand upright and bring one leg across your body, placing the ankle on the opposite knee. Gently push the crossed knee outward with your hand to feel a stretch in the glute of the crossed leg.
----
Supine Figure-Four Stretch: Lie on your back with both knees bent. Cross one ankle over the opposite knee and gently pull the uncrossed leg toward your chest, feeling the stretch in the glute of the crossed leg.
