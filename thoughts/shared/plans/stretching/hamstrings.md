Hamstring Stretches
Standing Hamstring Stretch: Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart. Hinge forward at the hips, keeping your back straight, and reach towards your toes until you feel a stretch in the back of your thighs.
----
Seated Forward Bend: Sit on the floor with your legs extended straight in front of you. Hinge forward at the hips, reaching towards your toes while keeping your back straight to feel the stretch in your hamstrings.
----
Supine Hamstring Stretch: Lie on your back and lift one leg up, keeping it straight. Gently pull the leg towards you by the thigh to stretch the hamstrings.
----
Standing One-Leg Hamstring Stretch: Stand on one leg and extend the other leg straight in front of you with the heel on the ground. Hinge forward at the hips to stretch the hamstring of the standing leg.
