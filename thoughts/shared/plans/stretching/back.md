Cat-Cow Stretch: Start on all fours with hands under shoulders and knees under hips. Inhale to arch your back (Cow), lifting your head and tailbone. Exhale to round your spine (Cat), tucking your chin and pelvis, stretching the lower back.

Knees-to-Chest Stretch: Lie on your back and bring both knees towards your chest. Hug your knees with your arms, gently pulling them closer to deepen the stretch in your lower back.
----
Cobra Stretch: Lie face down with your hands placed under your shoulders. Push your upper body off the ground by straightening your arms, lifting your chest while keeping your hips on the floor, to stretch the lower back.
----
Supine Spinal Twist: Lie on your back with arms extended out to the sides. Bend one knee and cross it over to the opposite side of your body, keeping your shoulders flat on the ground, to stretch the lower back and spine.
