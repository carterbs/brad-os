Side-Lying Quad Stretch: Lie on your side, bend your top knee, and grab your ankle to pull your heel towards your buttocks, stretching the quad.

Reverse Lunge Quad Stretch: Step one foot back into a reverse lunge, keeping your torso upright, and gently pull your back foot towards your buttocks to stretch the quad.
