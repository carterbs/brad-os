Neck Stretches
Neck Forward Tilt: Gently lower your chin toward your chest, feeling a stretch along the back of your neck.
----
Neck Side Tilt: Tilt your head to one side, bringing your ear toward your shoulder, stretching the side of your neck.
----
Neck Rotation: Slowly turn your head to one side, as if looking over your shoulder, to stretch the neck muscles.
----
Upper Trapezius Stretch: Sit or stand upright. Tilt your head to one side and gently pull your opposite arm down and away from your body to deepen the stretch in the upper trapezius.
----
Levator Scapulae Stretch: Sit or stand upright. Tilt your head diagonally downward toward your armpit and use your opposite hand to gently apply pressure to your head, stretching the levator scapulae muscle on the side of your neck.
