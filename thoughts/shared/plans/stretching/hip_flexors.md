Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch: Kneel on one knee with the other foot flat in front, creating a 90-degree angle. Gently push your hips forward while keeping your back straight to feel a stretch in the front of the kneeling legâ€™s hip.

Standing Lunge Hip Flexor Stretch: Step one foot forward into a lunge position, keeping the back leg straight and the heel on the ground. Push your hips forward to stretch the hip flexors of the back leg.
----
Pigeon Pose Stretch: From a plank position, bring one knee forward and place it behind your wrist, extending the opposite leg straight back. Lower your hips towards the ground to stretch the hip flexors and glutes.
----
High Knee Stretch: Stand tall and lift one knee towards your chest, holding it with your hand and gently pulling to deepen the stretch in the hip flexors.
----
Reverse Lunge Hip Flexor Stretch: Step one foot back into a reverse lunge, keeping your torso upright, and gently push your hips forward to stretch the hip flexors of the back leg.
